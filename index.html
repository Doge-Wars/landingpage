RISE OF THE DOGEWALKER

It is a period of civil war. Rebel tokens fueled by fomo seek to challenge the authority of DOGE. During the bear market of 2022, a new faction emerged, the LAIKA army. Initially seeking to challenge the SHIB terrorists, it has been discovered they now plan to eradicate every doge in the galaxy, funded by none other than the evil SEC Empire.

The fate of dogekind now lies in the hands of Elon Dogewalker. Our only hope for survival, he has ventured into space to transform the Republic space station into a weapon to protect planet doge, and destroy the LAKIA rebels and the SEC once and for all.

Pursued by the SEC's sinister LAIKA army, Dogewalker now travels the galaxy, extracting dogium from asteroids to upgrade the Doge Republic space station, saving dogekind from the tyranny of the SEC and restoring freedom to the doge galaxy…
